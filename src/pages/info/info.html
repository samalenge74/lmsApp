Help LMS App LMS or Leave Management System App enables employees to apply, view or cancel leaves from their IOS or Android Smartphones and Ipads or Android Tablets. Things To Know
  1. When applying for leave:
    • The Full/Half Day, Start Time and End Time fields are applicable when the request is for 1 (one) day only.
    • The Start time and End time fields are auto-populated based on the selection in the Full/Half Day field.
    • When the request is for more than 1 (one) day, the Start Time and End Time fields get auto-populated with 00:00:00.
    • To check your leave history, enter the start date and end date then click the submit button. The App will display your leave history for all leave types for the period between the dates chosen. Use the filter button to filter between leave types.
  2. The Leave Balance is updated/changed only after the request submitted has been approved.
  3. And also upon successfull cancellation, the Leave Balance is updated/changed.
  4. To cancel leave, slide the leave item to the left and click the delete button.
  5. When changing your password, slide the leave item to the left and click the delete button.
